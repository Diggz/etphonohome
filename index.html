This app was built by Diggz as part of the 2013 International Space Apps Challenge in Krakow, Poland. This uses technology by Voxeo Labs, including Tropo (to receive and record the phone messages) and Phono (for calling via the web browser and WebRTC integration) messages will be stored for later transmission into outer space by a big giant transmitter like this one.
